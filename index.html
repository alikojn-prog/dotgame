How to play
Four steps. You'll have it after one round.
1
Roll the dice
On your turn, tap the dice. The number you roll is how many lines you can draw.
2
Connect two dots
Tap one dot then another — or drag between them. Lines can't cross or clip another dot.
3
Close a triangle
Three dots joined by three lines becomes your triangle. It fills with your colour and scores a point.
4
Most triangles wins
The round ends when no more lines can be drawn. Whoever claimed the most triangles wins.
