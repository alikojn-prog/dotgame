How to play

Goal: claim more triangles than your opponent.

• Two players take turns. An opening roll-off decides who starts — highest roll goes first.
• On your turn, roll the dice by shaking your phone or tapping Tap to roll.
• The number you roll is how many lines you may draw. Connect two dots by tapping one then the other, or by dragging from one dot to the other.
• Lines can't cross another line or pass through a dot.
• Close a triangle (three dots joined by three lines) to claim it — your logo fills it and your Score goes up by one.
• The game ends when every dot has at least one line. Most triangles wins!

💡 Tip: Tap Tap to roll or press Space to roll the dice.
