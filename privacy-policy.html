Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

doTriangle ("the app") is developed and operated by Jani. This policy explains what information the app collects and how it is used.

Information We Collect

doTriangle does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app does not require account registration or sign-in.

Online Multiplayer

When you use the online multiplayer feature, the app connects to a game server via WebSocket to facilitate real-time gameplay. Only the data necessary for gameplay (room code, in-game moves) is transmitted. No personal identifiers are stored on the server.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children's Privacy

doTriangle does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
