## 1. App Store Listing

### App Name
**doTriangle – Triangle Puzzle Duel**

### Subtitle (30 chars max)
*Dots. Lines. Triangles. Win.*

### Description

doTriangle is the ultimate dots-and-triangles strategy game — beautifully designed, deceptively deep, and endlessly replayable.

Connect dots, close triangles, and claim your territory before your opponent does. Every line you draw is a calculated risk: set up your triangles or hand one to your rival?

**Three ways to play:**
- **vs CPU** — take on the AI at Easy, Medium, or Hard difficulty
- **Online vs Friend** — create a room, share a 6-character code, and battle in real time
- **Pass & Play** — two players, one phone

**Three board sizes:**
- Quick (10 dots) — a sharp few minutes
- Classic (20 dots) — the sweet spot
- Marathon (40 dots) — long, tactical, decisive

**Why you'll love it:**
- Zero ads. Zero tracking. Zero accounts needed.
- Sleek Nebula design — dark, vivid, and gorgeous on OLED screens.
- Instant online multiplayer with no sign-up required.
- Simple to learn, hard to master.

### Keywords (100 chars)

### App Category
**Primary:** Games → Puzzle  
**Secondary:** Games → Strategy

## 6. App Store Optimization (ongoing)

- **Screenshots:** Show the board mid-game, the win screen with confetti, and the online room-code screen. Use device frames. Dark background plays to the Nebula theme.
- **Preview video:** 15–30 sec screen recording of a satisfying game from start to win. No narration needed — let the game speak.
- **Ratings prompt:** Add `SKStoreReviewRequestAPI` in-game after a win (iOS will show the native prompt). Even 10 early ratings lifts search ranking significantly.
- **Respond to every early review** — App Store surfaces devs who engage.
